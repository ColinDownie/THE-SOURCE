# Health & Safety Overview  
_Version 1.0_

## Purpose

This document outlines the safety considerations for operating **THE SOURCE** in a public exhibition environment.

---

# Mechanical Safety

- all rotating components fully enclosed  
- no exposed pinch points  
- motor torque limited to safe thresholds  
- emergency stop integrated into staff console  

---

# Electrical Safety

- low‑voltage LED systems  
- isolated power supplies  
- surge protection  
- cable management compliant with museum standards  

---

# Visitor Safety

- stable plinth with anti‑tip design  
- headphone cables routed to avoid tripping  
- clear signage for interaction boundaries  
- accessible height for all visitors  

---

# Fire Safety

- flame‑retardant materials  
- no heat‑generating components near structure  
- compliance with venue fire code  
- staff trained in evacuation protocol  

---

# Summary

The Everlight is engineered to be safe, stable, and compliant with museum‑grade standards while maintaining its mythic presence.
