# Scene 12 — Stand‑Up Comedy Night  
_Version 1.0_

## Purpose

A Tiny comedian performs at the subterranean comedy club.  
The humour is chaotic, self‑referential, and full of squeaky punchlines.

---

# Script (Draft)

*(Tiny crowd chatter. A spotlight click.)*

**Tiny Comedian:**  
“GOOD EVENING, FUN‑SIZED FRIENDS!  
SO… THE BAKERY IS ON FIRE AGAIN!”

*(Tinies laugh.)*

**Tiny Comedian:**  
“AND THE TRAIN?  
STILL IN THE BAKERY!”

*(More laughter.)*

**Narrator:**  
“Why is this funny to them?”

**Tiny Comedian:**  
“BECAUSE IF WE DON’T LAUGH, WE’LL CRY!”

*(Tinies cheer.)*

**Z:**  
“Humour is a sacred flame.”

**Narrator:**  
“Please don’t encourage them.”
