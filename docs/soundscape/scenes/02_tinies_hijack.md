# Scene 02 — The Tinies Hijack the Commentary  
_Version 1.0_

## Purpose

This scene marks the moment the Tinies steal the narrator’s voice modifier and begin speaking in modulated, comprehensible voices.  
It’s the comedic turning point where the soundscape shifts from mythic calm to full chaos.

---

# Script (Draft)

**Narrator (calm, trying to continue):**  
“As we observe the Everlight’s inner—”

*(Ultrasonic squeaks erupt.)*

**Narrator:**  
“No, please don’t—  
That device is not for—  
Oh dear.”

*(A loud *CLICK*. The Tinies’ voices suddenly become modulated and understandable.)*

**Tiny #1 (excited):**  
“HELLOOOO GIANTS!”

**Tiny #2:**  
“WE HAVE A VOICE!  
WE HAVE A VOICE!”

**Narrator:**  
“Please return that.  
It is extremely delicate.”

**Tiny #3:**  
“WE ARE DELICATE!”

*(Tinies cheer.)*

**Narrator (sighing):**  
“This is not how the commentary is meant to proceed.”

**Tiny #1:**  
“WE’RE IMPROVING IT!”

---

# Notes

- This is the first time visitors hear the Tinies in full voice.  
- The narrator’s dignity begins to crumble.  
- The Tinies are ecstatic, chaotic, and unstoppable.  
- Z does not intervene yet.
