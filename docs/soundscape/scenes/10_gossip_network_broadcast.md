# Scene 10 — Gossip Network Broadcast  
_Version 1.0_

## Purpose

Tilda Tines broadcasts the latest subterranean gossip.  
It’s fast, chaotic, and wildly inaccurate.

---

# Script (Draft)

*(A tiny radio jingle plays.)*

**Tilda Tines (rapid‑fire):**  
“WELCOME TO TINY TALK RADIO!  
TODAY’S HEADLINES:  
THE BAKERY IS STILL ON FIRE,  
THE TRAIN IS STILL IN THE BAKERY,  
AND SOMEONE SAW THE NARRATOR BLINK TWICE —  
WHAT DOES IT MEAN?”

**Narrator:**  
“It means I blinked.”

**Tilda:**  
“SCANDALOUS!”

*(Tinies gasp.)*

**Tilda:**  
“ALSO:  
A SPOON WAS DROPPED.  
THE PILLARS ARE LOSING THEIR MINDS.”

*(Deep rumble.)*

**Pillar:**  
“THE OMEN…  
IS TRUE.”

**Narrator:**  
“It is not an omen.”

---

# Notes

- Tilda speaks extremely fast.  
- The Pillars misinterpret everything.
