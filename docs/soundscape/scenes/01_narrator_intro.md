# Scene 01 — Narrator Introduction  
_Version 1.0_

## Purpose

This scene introduces:

- the narrator  
- the Everlight  
- the calm, mythic tone  
- the first hint of Tiny interference  

It sets up the contrast between serenity and chaos.

---

# Script (Draft)

**Narrator (calm, mythic):**  
“Welcome to the Everlight.  
A structure of luminous balance, cosmic harmony, and—  
One moment…”

*(Ultrasonic squeaking begins.)*

**Narrator:**  
“Yes, I can hear you.  
No, please don’t touch that—  
Oh dear.”

*(Squeaks intensify.)*

**Narrator:**  
“Ladies and gentlemen, I apologise.  
The… inhabitants… beneath the structure are excited today.”

*(A loud squeak.)*

**Narrator:**  
“No, I am not telling them that.  
It’s rude.”

*(Squeaks of protest.)*

**Narrator (sighing):**  
“Very well.  
They would like you to know they are ‘doing their best under difficult circumstances.’”

---

# Notes

- This scene should feel calm → interrupted → chaotic → resigned.  
- The Tinies remain ultrasonic for now.  
- The narrator stays dignified but strained.  
- Z and the Pillars do not appear yet.
