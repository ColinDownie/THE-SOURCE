# The Narrator — Character Profile  
_Version 1.0_

## Introduction

The Narrator is the dignified, poetic, mythic voice of **THE SOURCE** — or at least, he tries to be.  
He is the audience’s guide to the Everlight, the Tinies, and the mythic cosmology.  
He is also the Tinies’ favourite target.

This document defines:

- his tone  
- his personality  
- his comedic role  
- how he interacts with the Tinies, Z, and the Pillars  
- sample lines  
- how he collapses under pressure  

The narrator is the **straight man** in a universe of chaos.

---

# Core Traits

The Narrator is:

- calm  
- dignified  
- poetic  
- authoritative  
- mythic  
- patient (to a point)  
- increasingly exasperated  
- constantly interrupted  
- desperately trying to maintain control  

His voice evokes:

- Morgan Freeman  
- David Attenborough  
- James Earl Jones  
- A weary museum audio guide who has seen too much  

He is the tonal anchor of the exhibit.

---

# Narrative Function

The Narrator provides:

- exposition  
- mythic framing  
- structural clarity  
- emotional grounding  
- comedic contrast  

He attempts to deliver a serious, elegant commentary about:

- the Everlight  
- the cosmic entities  
- the Tinies’ civilisation  
- the exhibit’s themes  

But the Tinies constantly derail him.
