# Scene 16 — Claims Department Breakdown  
_Version 1.0_

## Purpose

This scene reveals the bureaucratic heart of the Tinies’ civilisation:  
the Claims Department, where paperwork goes to die.

---

# Script (Draft)

*(Stacks of paper rustle. A stressed Tiny mutters legal jargon.)*

**Lint (Claims Specialist):**  
“I HAVE BEEN WORKING ON THIS FORM…  
FOR FOURTEEN YEARS.”

**Tiny #1:**  
“HOW MANY PAGES ARE LEFT?”

**Lint:**  
“YES.”

**Narrator:**  
“That’s not an answer.”

**Lint:**  
“NEITHER IS THIS FORM.”

*(Tinies groan. A stamp slams repeatedly.)*

**Tiny #2:**  
“WE NEED MORE INK!”

**Tiny #3:**  
“WE ATE THE LAST CARTRIDGE!”

**Narrator:**  
“…Why?”

**Tiny #3:**  
“IT LOOKED LIKE A SNACK!”

*(Z hums gently.)*

**Z:**  
“Paperwork is a journey of the soul.”

**Lint:**  
“MY SOUL IS TIRED.”
