# Scene 06 — The Union Meeting  
_Version 1.0_

## Purpose

This scene reveals the Tinies’ intense union culture.  
It’s chaotic, loud, and full of overlapping demands — a comedic contrast to the Everlight’s serene exterior.

---

# Script (Draft)

*(A tiny gavel bangs. Multiple squeaks.)*

**Union Rep Tiny:**  
“ORDER! ORDER!  
THE MEETING OF T.U.F.F. — TINIES UNITED FOR FAIRNESS — IS NOW IN SESSION!”

**Tiny #1:**  
“WE DEMAND SAFETY RAILINGS!”

**Tiny #2:**  
“WE DEMAND SNACK BREAKS!”

**Tiny #3:**  
“WE DEMAND A SECOND BAKERY!”

**Narrator:**  
“You already have a bakery.  
It is currently on fire.”

**Tiny #3:**  
“EXACTLY!”

*(Tinies cheer.)*

**Union Rep Tiny:**  
“WE ALSO DEMAND THE INSURANCE CLAIM BE RESOLVED!”

**Narrator:**  
“I have no authority over that.”

**Union Rep Tiny:**  
“THEN WHY ARE YOU HERE?”

**Narrator:**  
“I ask myself that daily.”

*(Z enters with a harmonic tone.)*

**Z:**  
“Little Ones…  
Your voices are heard.”

*(Tinies cheer louder.)*

---

# Notes

- Overlapping voices create comedic density.  
- The narrator is exhausted.  
- Z remains serene.  
- The Pillars do not appear in this scene.
