# Risk Assessment  
_Version 1.0_

## Purpose

This document identifies potential risks associated with the Everlight installation and outlines mitigation strategies.

---

# Mechanical Risks

### Moving Parts
- **Risk:** pinch points  
- **Mitigation:** full enclosure  

### Motor Failure
- **Risk:** sudden stop  
- **Mitigation:** torque limiter + soft‑stop logic  

### Bearing Wear
- **Risk:** wobble or noise  
- **Mitigation:** scheduled maintenance  

---

# Electrical Risks

### Power Surge
- **Risk:** LED or motor damage  
- **Mitigation:** surge protection  

### Overheating
- **Risk:** component failure  
- **Mitigation:** thermal cutoff  

---

# Visitor Risks

### Trip Hazards
- **Mitigation:** cable routing + clear pathways  

### Touching Sculpture
- **Mitigation:** plinth height + signage  

---

# Summary

The Everlight is engineered to minimise risk while maintaining its mythic presence.
