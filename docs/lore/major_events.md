# Major Events  
_Version 1.0_

## Purpose

A deeper look at the defining events that shaped the Everlight’s hidden civilisation.

---

# Events

### The Dropping of the Amazeball  
The accidental moment that created the first Tiny.  
Considered sacred by some, annoying by others.

### The First Bakery Fire  
A turning point in Tiny culture.  
Led to the founding of the Claims Department.

### The Great Rail Disaster  
A train derailed into the bakery.  
Tinies still argue about whose fault it was.

### The Awakening of Z  
A harmonic shift that brought cosmic calm.  
Tinies immediately asked Z for snacks.

### The Spoon Incident  
A spoon was dropped.  
The Pillars declared a prophecy.  
The narrator disagreed.

### The Union Uprising  
Tinies demanded snack breaks.  
They won.

---

# Summary

These events form the backbone of Tiny identity — chaotic, comedic, and strangely profound.
