# THE EVERLIGHT — Conceptual Design Document  
_Version 1.0_

## Overview

**The Everlight** is the central sculptural element of **THE SOURCE**, a conceptual artwork that merges kinetic sculpture, mythic cosmology, immersive sound design, and comedic narrative. It is both a physical installation and the visible tip of a much larger, unseen universe.

Visitors encounter a serene, luminous, rotating structure.  
Through headphones, they discover the chaotic, bureaucratic micro‑civilisation living beneath it.

The Everlight is the heart of the exhibit — visually mythic, sonically alive, and narratively inhabited.

---

## The Physical Sculpture

The Everlight is:

- a multi‑rod kinetic structure  
- rotating on multiple axes  
- illuminated from within  
- engineered with precision  
- symbolic of cosmic order  
- serene, elegant, and meditative  

Its visual language is:

- mythic  
- geometric  
- celestial  
- architectural  
- ritualistic  

The sculpture is intentionally calm.  
It contrasts sharply with the chaos beneath it.

---

## The Hidden Universe Beneath

Unseen by visitors, beneath the Everlight lies a full subterranean civilisation known as **the Tinies**.

They are:

- tiny  
- shiny  
- squeaky  
- furious  
- bureaucratic  
- unionised  
- trapped  

They were accidentally created when the original Amazeball was dropped during installation, triggering a catastrophic insurance dispute that has lasted years.

Until the claim is settled, the Tinies cannot leave.

They have built:

- homes  
- families  
- unions  
- a council  
- a bakery  
- a pub  
- a rail network  
- a legal office  
- a health & safety department  
- a soap opera  
- a stand‑up comedy night  

They are the unseen soundscape and mischief makers of the Everlight.

---

## The Insurance Claim (The Eternal Curse)

The Tinies’ entire existence is defined by a single unresolved event:

**The Amazeball was dropped.**

The artist blames the museum.  
The museum blames the delivery driver.  
The driver blames the packaging.  
The packaging blames the artist.  
The insurance company blames everyone.

The claim is “in review”.  
It has been “in review” for decades.

The Tinies are trapped in bureaucratic limbo until the claim is settled.

This is the comedic and mythic engine of the entire universe.

---

## Z — The Central Consciousness

Z is the Everlight’s internal intelligence.

Z is:

- calm  
- resonant  
- cosmic  
- patient  
- wise  
- deeply respectful of the Tinies  

Z can speak directly to the Tinies.  
Z reveres them as sacred custodians of the Everlight’s inner workings.

The Tinies respond with:

> “WE’RE NOT SACRED. WE’RE TIRED.”

---

## The Pillars — Structural Spirits

The Pillars are the ancient vertical entities that support the Everlight.

They are:

- slow  
- deep  
- resonant  
- ceremonial  
- reverent toward the Tinies  

They call the Tinies:

- “The Little Keepers”  
- “The Squeaks of Destiny”  
- “The Custodians of the Glow”  

The Tinies call the Pillars:

> “VERY TALL. VERY DRAMATIC. VERY USELESS.”

---

## The Narrator — The Straight Man

A Morgan‑Freeman‑style narrator provides the official commentary.

He is:

- dignified  
- poetic  
- mythic  
- calm  
- constantly interrupted  

He is the only one who can “interpret” the Tinies’ ultrasonic speech.

But the Tinies constantly:

- squeal  
- interrupt  
- heckle  
- steal his voice modifier  
- hijack the show  

This becomes the exhibit’s signature comedic rhythm.

---

## The Soundscape

Visitors wear headphones and hear:

- the narrator  
- the Tinies (ultrasonic)  
- the Tinies (modulated, after stealing the device)  
- Z  
- the Pillars  
- the subterranean village  
- the soap opera  
- the stand‑up comedy night  
- direct interaction with visitors and staff  

The soundscape is layered, alive, and constantly shifting.

---

## Audience Interaction

The Tinies speak directly to visitors:

- heckling  
- warning  
- gossiping  
- complaining  
- flirting  
- panicking  
- performing  

They also interact with staff:

- demanding meetings  
- unionising them  
- accusing them of sabotage  
- begging for help with the claim  

This makes the exhibit feel alive and reactive.

---

## The Tinies’ Role in the Universe

They are:

- the unseen soundscape  
- the mischief makers  
- the bureaucratic gremlins  
- the sacred custodians (according to Z and the Pillars)  
- the comedic soul of the exhibit  
- the trapped civilisation waiting for justice  

They are the heartbeat of THE SOURCE.

---

## Concept Summary

**The Everlight is a luminous kinetic sculpture containing a trapped, squeaky, bureaucratic micro‑civilisation who interact with visitors and staff, revered by cosmic entities, and held in limbo until their ancient insurance claim is finally settled.**

This document defines the conceptual foundation of the Everlight and its hidden universe.
