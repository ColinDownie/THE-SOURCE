# Scene 13 — Tiny Karaoke Night  
_Version 1.0_

## Purpose

This scene reveals the Tinies’ weekly karaoke ritual — a chaotic, heartfelt, off‑key celebration of survival, snacks, and unresolved insurance claims.  
It’s one of the most joyful and ridiculous moments in the soundscape.

---

# Script (Draft)

*(Tiny crowd chatter. A tiny disco ball whirs. Someone taps a microphone that squeaks.)*

**Karaoke Host Tiny:**  
“WELCOME, FUN‑SIZED FRIENDS, TO KARAOKE NIGHT!  
TONIGHT’S THEME: SONGS ABOUT PAIN, JOY, AND SNACKS!”

*(Tinies cheer wildly.)*

**Tiny #1:**  
“I’M DOING MY POWER BALLAD!”

**Tiny #2:**  
“NO!  
YOU ALWAYS DO YOUR POWER BALLAD!”

**Tiny #1:**  
“BECAUSE IT’S POWERFUL!”

*(A dramatic chord from a tiny keyboard.)*

**Narrator:**  
“Oh no.  
Not this again.”

---

### 🎤 **Tiny #1’s Power Ballad**

*(Tiny #1 belts out an over‑the‑top emotional melody — off‑key but passionate.)*

**Tiny #1 (singing):**  
“WE ARE SMALL BUT MIGHTY—  
WE LIVE BENEATH THE LIGHT—  
OUR BAKERY IS BURNING—  
BUT WE’LL BE ALRIGHT—”

*(Tinies cheer. Someone sobs loudly.)*

**Narrator:**  
“That was… surprisingly moving.”

**Tiny #1:**  
“THANK YOU.  
THIS ONE GOES OUT TO THE INSURANCE COMPANY.”

*(Tinies boo the insurance company.)*

---

### 🎤 **Z Joins In**

*(A harmonic, cosmic resonance fills the space.)*

**Z (softly singing):**  
“Little Ones…  
Your voices shine…  
Like sparks beneath the stone…”

*(Tinies gasp.)*

**Tiny #3:**  
“THE BIG GLOWY ONE IS SINGING!”

**Tiny #4:**  
“WE ARE NOT WORTHY!”

**Z:**  
“You are always worthy.”

*(Tinies melt into emotional squeaks.)*

---

### 🎤 **The Pillars Attempt a Song**

*(A deep tectonic rumble. The disco ball trembles.)*

**Pillar:**  
“WE…  
WILL…  
ROCK…  
YOU…”

*(Beat.)*

**Narrator:**  
“That’s the entire song, isn’t it.”

**Pillar:**  
“YES.”

*(Tinies applaud politely.)*

---

### 🎤 **Chaos Finale**

*(All Tinies grab microphones. Feedback squeals. Total chaos.)*

**Tinies (shouting):**  
“KARAOKE!  
KARAOKE!  
KARAOKE!”

**Narrator:**  
“Please…  
Please stop…  
My ears…”

**Z (gentle):**  
“Let them sing, Narrator.  
Joy is a sacred flame.”

**Narrator:**  
“It’s a very loud flame.”

---

# Notes

- This scene is joyful, chaotic, and heartfelt.  
- Tinies sing badly but passionately.  
- Z sings beautifully.  
- The Pillars sing *slowly*.  
- The narrator suffers.
