# Visitor Journey — Detailed Breakdown  
_Version 1.0_

## Introduction

This document expands the visitor journey into a step‑by‑step experiential flow, showing how the exhibit unfolds emotionally, narratively, and sonically.

---

# 1. **Approach — The Silent Myth**

Visitors see:

- a serene, glowing sculpture  
- slow rotation  
- architectural elegance  
- no hint of chaos  

Emotion: **awe, curiosity**

---

# 2. **Headphones On — The Mythic Frame**

The narrator begins:

- calm  
- poetic  
- authoritative  

Z may hum faintly in the background.

Emotion: **wonder, immersion**

---

# 3. **First Interruption — The Squeak**

Ultrasonic Tiny squeaks disrupt the narration.

Visitors realise:

- something is alive  
- something is beneath the sculpture  
- something is trying to communicate  

Emotion: **surprise, humour**

---

# 4. **The Hijack — Tiny Voices Emerge**

Tinies steal the narrator’s device.

Their voices become:

- modulated  
- chaotic  
- excited  

Emotion: **delight, confusion**

---

# 5. **Cosmic Expansion — Z and the Pillars**

Z speaks:

- calm  
- harmonic  
- wise  

The Pillars speak:

- slow  
- booming  
- ceremonial  

Emotion: **mythic awe**

---

# 6. **Civilisation Reveal — Full Chaos**

Visitors hear:

- bakery fire  
- trains  
- gossip  
- union meetings  
- arguments  
- celebrations  

Emotion: **laughter, immersion, discovery**

---

# 7. **Narrator Collapse — The Breaking Point**

The narrator tries to regain control.  
The Tinies refuse.  
Z offers cosmic reassurance.  
The Pillars declare omens.

Emotion: **comedy, empathy**

---

# 8. **Cosmic Closure — The Final Tone**

Z delivers a final reflective moment.  
The narrator sighs.  
The Tinies interrupt one last time.

Emotion: **warmth, humour, resonance**

---

# Summary

The visitor journey moves from serenity → curiosity → chaos → myth → comedy → closure.  
It mirrors the structure of the universe itself.
