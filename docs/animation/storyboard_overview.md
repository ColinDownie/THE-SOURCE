# Storyboard Overview  
_Version 1.0_

## Purpose

This document outlines the visual structure of the concept animation for **THE SOURCE** — a short film that introduces the Everlight, the Tinies, Z, and the mythic architecture of the installation.

---

# Structure

### 1. Opening Shot — The Everlight  
- slow rotation  
- soft glow  
- serene ambience  
- narrator begins calm introduction  

### 2. First Disturbance  
- tiny ultrasonic squeaks  
- narrator falters  
- subtle camera push-in  

### 3. Chaos Emerges  
- Tinies steal the voice modifier  
- modulated voices burst in  
- comedic timing  

### 4. Cosmic Expansion  
- Z appears as a harmonic light form  
- Pillars rumble in the deep  
- camera widens to reveal mythic scale  

### 5. Civilisation Montage  
- bakery fire  
- trains  
- union meeting  
- gossip network  
- tiny soap opera  

### 6. Narrator Breaking Point  
- comedic meltdown  
- Tinies unfazed  

### 7. Cosmic Closure  
- Z restores harmony  
- Everlight glows warmly  
- fade to black  

---

# Summary

The storyboard balances serenity, chaos, and cosmic humour — mirroring the exhibit itself.
