# Soundscape Overview  
_Version 1.0_

## Introduction

The soundscape is the beating heart of **THE SOURCE**.  
It reveals the hidden civilisation beneath the Everlight and connects the physical, subterranean, and mythic planes.

This document outlines:

- the soundscape structure  
- the layers of audio  
- the characters’ roles  
- the tonal progression  
- how visitors experience the universe  

---

# Soundscape Layers

The soundscape operates on three simultaneous layers:

## 1. The Physical Layer  
- subtle mechanical hums  
- rotational sounds  
- soft illumination pulses  
- structural resonance  

## 2. The Subterranean Layer  
- Tinies squeaking  
- chatter, arguments, gossip  
- trains, tools, alarms  
- bakery chaos  
- union meetings  
- soap opera recordings  
- emergency squeal sirens  

## 3. The Mythic Layer  
- Z’s harmonic voice  
- the Pillars’ tectonic tones  
- cosmic resonance  
- ritual chants  
- structural harmonics  

These layers blend dynamically.

---

# Tonal Progression

The soundscape follows a loose narrative arc:

1. **Calm introduction**  
   - narrator sets the tone  
   - Z offers cosmic context  

2. **First Tiny interruption**  
   - ultrasonic squeaks  
   - narrator struggles  

3. **Chaos escalation**  
   - Tinies steal the voice modifier  
   - modulated Tiny voices appear  

4. **Mythic expansion**  
   - Pillars speak  
   - Z mediates  

5. **Full civilisation reveal**  
   - bakery fire  
   - union meeting  
   - rail network meltdown  

6. **Cosmic reflection**  
   - Z offers harmony  
   - narrator regains composure  

7. **Final comedic collapse**  
   - Tinies interrupt the ending  
   - narrator gives up  

---

# Interaction with Visitors

The Tinies:

- address visitors directly  
- warn them  
- flirt with them  
- accuse them of sabotage  
- ask for help with the claim  

The narrator tries to maintain dignity.  
Z tries to maintain harmony.  
The Pillars misinterpret everything.

---

# Summary

The soundscape is a living, breathing ecosystem that reveals the Everlight’s hidden universe.  
It blends comedy, myth, chaos, and serenity into a single immersive experience.
