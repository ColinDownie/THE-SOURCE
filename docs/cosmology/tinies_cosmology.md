# The Tinies — Cosmology & Origin  
_Version 1.0_

## Introduction

The Tinies are the chaotic, comedic, bureaucratic heart of **THE SOURCE**.  
They are a full subterranean civilisation living beneath the Everlight, unseen by visitors but fully alive in the soundscape.

This document defines:

- their origin  
- their cosmological role  
- their relationship with cosmic entities  
- their bureaucracy  
- their mythic misunderstandings  
- their eternal curse  

The Tinies are both the **comic engine** and the **mythic paradox** of the universe.

# Origin Myth — The Dropped Amazeball

The Tinies were born from a single catastrophic event:

**The Amazeball was dropped during installation.**

This moment created:

- a shockwave of bureaucratic energy  
- a cascade of blame  
- a multi‑party insurance dispute  
- a metaphysical rupture beneath the sculpture  
- the spontaneous emergence of the Tinies  

They are, in essence:

- the physical manifestation of bureaucratic fallout  
- the children of paperwork  
- the offspring of negligence  
- the spirits of unresolved claims  

Their existence is tied to the insurance claim.  
Until it is settled, they cannot leave.
