# Motor Requirements  
_Version 1.0_

## Purpose

This document defines the motor specifications required to achieve the Everlight’s slow, meditative rotation.

---

# Performance Requirements

### Rotation Speed
- target: 1–3 RPM  
- smooth, continuous motion  
- no visible stepping  

### Torque
- sufficient to drive multi‑rod assembly  
- safety margin of 30%  
- low vibration output  

### Noise Level
- near‑silent operation  
- < 25 dB preferred  
- no audible hum  

---

# Electrical Requirements

- low‑voltage DC motor  
- integrated speed controller  
- surge protection  
- thermal cutoff  

---

# Mounting Requirements

- vibration‑isolated base  
- accessible for maintenance  
- enclosed for visitor safety  

---

# Summary

The motor must be quiet, reliable, and capable of producing a serene, uninterrupted rotation.
