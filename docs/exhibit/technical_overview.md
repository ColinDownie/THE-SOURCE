## Purpose

This document outlines the physical, mechanical, and experiential architecture of **THE SOURCE**.

The exhibit is designed as a **three‑layered encounter**:

1. **Visual Layer**  
   The Everlight rotates with meditative calm.

2. **Auditory Layer**

3. **Mythic Layer**  
   Z, the Pillars, and the narrator provide cosmic framing.
