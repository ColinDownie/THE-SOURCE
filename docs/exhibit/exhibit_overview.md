# Exhibit Overview  
_Version 1.0_

## Introduction

This document outlines the physical, experiential, and narrative structure of **THE SOURCE** as a museum‑scale installation.  
It describes how visitors encounter the Everlight, how the soundscape unfolds, and how the hidden universe is revealed.

---

# Physical Components

## The Everlight Sculpture  
- multi‑rod kinetic structure  
- internal illumination  
- slow, meditative rotation  
- engineered precision  
- mythic visual language  

## The Subterranean Layer  
(Not visible to visitors)  
- the Tinies’ civilisation  
- miniature architecture  
- sound‑driven worldbuilding  

## The Audio System  
- directional headphones  
- layered soundscape  
- dynamic mixing  
- character‑driven scenes  

---

# Visitor Journey

### 1. **Approach**  
Visitors see a serene, glowing sculpture.  
No hint of chaos yet.

### 2. **Headphones On**  
The narrator begins a calm, mythic introduction.

### 3. **First Interruption**  
Ultrasonic Tiny squeaks disrupt the commentary.

### 4. **Chaos Emerges**  
Tinies steal the voice modifier.  
Modulated voices appear.

### 5. **Cosmic Expansion**  
Z speaks.  
The Pillars resonate.  
The universe opens.

### 6. **Civilisation Reveal**  
Visitors hear the bakery, the trains, the union meetings, the gossip network.

### 7. **Final Collapse**  
The narrator gives up.  
The Tinies celebrate.  
Z offers cosmic closure.

---

# Themes

- myth vs. bureaucracy  
- cosmic reverence vs. tiny chaos  
- serenity vs. squealing  
- the sacred vs. the mundane  
- the beauty of systems breaking down  

---

# Summary

The exhibit blends sculpture, sound, myth, comedy, and worldbuilding into a single immersive experience.  
Visitors encounter a serene object and discover an entire universe beneath it.
