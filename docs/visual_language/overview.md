# Visual Language Overview  
_Version 1.0_

## Purpose

This document defines the visual grammar of **THE SOURCE** —  
the symbolic, mythic, and structural language that underpins the Everlight and its hidden civilisation.

The visual language must feel:

- ancient yet engineered  
- mythic yet functional  
- elegant yet playful  
- cosmic yet tiny  

It bridges the serene sculpture above with the chaotic world below.

---

# Core Principles

### 1. Duality  
Every symbol expresses a tension:
- calm vs chaos  
- myth vs bureaucracy  
- cosmic vs mundane  
- light vs subterranean  

### 2. Modularity  
Symbols combine like components:
- rods  
- rings  
- nodes  
- pulses  
- flows  

### 3. Resonance  
Shapes echo the Everlight’s rotation:
- circular motifs  
- spirals  
- radiating lines  
- harmonic spacing  

### 4. Tiny Influence  
Hidden within the elegance:
- small imperfections  
- playful marks  
- tiny “signatures”  
- hints of mischief  

---

# Symbol Categories

### Cosmic Symbols  
Used by Z and the Pillars:
- concentric rings  
- harmonic waves  
- slow‑moving glyphs  

### Structural Symbols  
Used for engineering and architecture:
- rod diagrams  
- bearing marks  
- rotation arcs  

### Tiny Symbols  
Used by the subterranean civilisation:
- blocky stamps  
- snack icons  
- union badges  
- bakery warnings  

---

# Summary

The visual language is the connective tissue of the universe —  
a mythic grammar that unites sculpture, sound, narrative, and worldbuilding.
