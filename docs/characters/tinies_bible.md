# The Tinies — Character Bible  
_Version 1.0_

## Introduction

The Tinies are the chaotic, squeaky, bureaucratic micro‑civilisation living beneath the Everlight.  
They are the comedic engine of **THE SOURCE**, and this document defines:

- their personalities  
- their factions  
- their unions  
- their recurring characters  
- their comedic archetypes  
- their roles in the soundscape  
- how they interact with visitors, staff, Z, and the Pillars  

This is the definitive reference for writing, voicing, and designing the Tinies.

---

# Core Traits of All Tinies

All Tinies share certain universal characteristics:

- **Squeaky** (ultrasonic by default)  
- **Shiny** (slightly plasticine, slightly metallic)  
- **Tiny** (obviously)  
- **Perpetually stressed**  
- **Bureaucratically cursed**  
- **Unionised**  
- **Easily distracted**  
- **Easily outraged**  
- **Easily excited**  
- **Loud**  
- **Opinionated**  
- **Chaotic**  
- **Weirdly competent when it matters**  

They are a civilisation built on:

- paperwork  
- gossip  
- panic  
- improvisation  
- squealing  
- committee meetings  
- misplaced tools  
- accidental brilliance

# Tinies Society Structure

The Tinies have a surprisingly complex social structure:

## 1. The Council of Tiny Affairs  
A rotating committee of overworked Tinies who:

- debate everything  
- resolve nothing  
- argue constantly  
- take minutes no one reads  
- issue memos no one follows  

## 2. The Union(s)  
There are **multiple unions**, including:

- **UTU** — Union of Tiny Unionists  
- **TUG** — Tiny Underground Guild  
- **SQUEAL** — Society for Quality, Equity, and Labour  
- **T.U.F.F.** — Tinies United For Fairness  

Each union believes it is the *real* union.

## 3. The Departments  
The Tinies have departments such as:

- Health & Safety  
- Legal  
- Rail Operations  
- Gossip & Communications  
- Snack Distribution  
- Emergency Squeal Response  
- Claims & Complaints  
- Claims About Claims  
- Claims About Claims About Claims  

## 4. The Subterranean Village  
A fully functioning underground town with:

- a bakery  
- a pub  
- a rail network  
- a comedy club  
- a soap opera studio  
- a council chamber  
- a panic bunker  
- a lost‑and‑found (mostly lost)

# Recurring Characters

These characters appear throughout the soundscape and narrative.

## **1. Squeakerton** — Head of the Council  
- perpetually overwhelmed  
- speaks in rapid‑fire squeaks  
- constantly filing paperwork  
- believes the claim will be resolved “any day now”  
- it will not  

## **2. Tilda Tines** — Gossip Correspondent  
- knows everything  
- knows things she shouldn’t  
- knows things that haven’t happened yet  
- spreads rumours at ultrasonic speed  
- beloved and feared  

## **3. Grumble** — Health & Safety Officer  
- permanently stressed  
- carries a clipboard  
- shouts “THAT’S NOT SAFE!” at everything  
- including things
