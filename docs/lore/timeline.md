# Timeline of the Everlight Universe  
_Version 1.0_

## Purpose

A chronological overview of major events in the mythic and mundane history of the Everlight and its subterranean civilisation.

---

# Timeline

### • Before Time  
Z exists in the harmonic realm.  
The Pillars slumber in tectonic stillness.

### • The First Turning  
The Everlight is conceived — a cosmic idea waiting for form.

### • Installation Day  
The sculpture is assembled.  
A single dropped tool creates the first Tiny.

### • Day 1  
The Tinies discover snacks.  
Chaos begins.

### • Week 1  
The bakery is founded.  
The bakery catches fire.

### • Week 2  
The rail network is built.  
The rail network collapses.

### • Month 1  
The union forms.  
The claims department becomes overwhelmed.

### • Month 2  
Z appears for the first time.  
The Tinies scream with joy.

### • Month 3  
The Pillars awaken.  
They declare the Spoon Prophecy.

### • Present Day  
The Everlight turns.  
The Tinies thrive.  
The narrator suffers.  
Z hums.  
The Pillars rumble.  

---

# Summary

The timeline blends cosmic myth with tiny chaos — the essence of the Everlight universe.
