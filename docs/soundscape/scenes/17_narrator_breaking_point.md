# Scene 17 — The Narrator’s Breaking Point  
_Version 1.0_

## Purpose

This is the emotional peak of the narrator’s arc —  
the moment he finally snaps under the weight of Tiny chaos.

---

# Script (Draft)

*(Chaos everywhere: alarms, squeaks, rumbling Pillars.)*

**Narrator (strained):**  
“I am a professional.  
I have narrated documentaries, operas, planetary histories—”

*(Tinies interrupt.)*

**Tiny #1:**  
“CAN YOU NARRATE OUR SNACK BREAK?”

**Narrator:**  
“No.”

**Tiny #2:**  
“CAN YOU NARRATE THE BAKERY FIRE?”

**Narrator:**  
“No!”

**Tiny #3:**  
“CAN YOU NARRATE—”

**Narrator (finally snapping):**  
“NO!  
NO MORE NARRATING!  
NO MORE CHAOS!  
NO MORE SPOONS BEING DECLARED OMENS!”

*(A deep rumble.)*

**Pillar:**  
“THE SPOON…  
WAS…  
A SIGN.”

**Narrator:**  
“STOP SAYING THAT!”

*(Z enters, warm and calm.)*

**Z:**  
“Narrator…  
Breathe.”

**Narrator:**  
“I am breathing.  
I am breathing aggressively.”
