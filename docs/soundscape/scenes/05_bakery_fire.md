# Scene 05 — The Bakery Fire  
_Version 1.0_

## Purpose

This scene reveals the Tinies’ subterranean civilisation in full chaos.  
The bakery catches fire (again), and everyone panics except Z and the Pillars.

---

# Script (Draft)

*(Alarm bells ring. Tinies scream.)*

**Tiny #1:**  
“THE BAKERY IS ON FIRE!”

**Tiny #2:**  
“WHO PUT THE OVEN ON MAXIMUM?”

**Tiny #3:**  
“IT WAS A TEAM‑BUILDING EXERCISE!”

**Narrator (exasperated):**  
“Why is it always the bakery?”

*(More squeals. A small explosion.)*

**Tiny #4:**  
“THE SOURDOUGH HAS ACHIEVED SENTIENCE!”

**Narrator:**  
“That is not possible.”

**Tiny #4:**  
“IT IS CHASING US!”

*(Z enters, calm and harmonic.)*

**Z:**  
“Little Ones…  
Your flames burn brightly today.”

**Narrator:**  
“Z, this is not symbolic.  
This is an actual fire.”

*(A deep rumble — the Pillars speak.)*

**Pillar:**  
“THE SACRED FLAMES…  
ARE A SIGN.”

**Narrator:**  
“Please stop calling everything a sign.”

---

# Notes

- This is the first full “civilisation chaos” scene.  
- Tinies overlap, panic, and shout.  
- Z remains serene.  
- The Pillars remain dramatic.  
- The narrator is at breaking point.
