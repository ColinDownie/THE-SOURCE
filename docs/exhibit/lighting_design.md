# Lighting Design  
_Version 1.0_

## Purpose

This document describes the lighting philosophy and technical implementation for **THE SOURCE**.

---

# Lighting Goals

- evoke serenity  
- highlight rotational motion  
- create a soft, mythic glow  
- avoid distracting shadows  
- support the soundscape’s emotional arc  

---

# Components

### Internal LEDs
- warm white base tone  
- subtle pulsing tied to rotation  
- diffusion layer for even glow  

### External Lighting
- low‑intensity ambient wash  
- no harsh spotlights  
- colour temperature matched to sculpture  

---

# Behaviour

- slow breathing effect  
- brightness increases during cosmic scenes  
- soft dimming during chaotic Tiny scenes  
- final warm fade during closure  

---

# Summary

The lighting reinforces the Everlight’s dual nature:  
calm above, chaos below, harmony throughout.
