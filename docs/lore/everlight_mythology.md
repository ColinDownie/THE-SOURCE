# Everlight Mythology  
_Version 1.0_

## Purpose

A mythic framework that explains the Everlight’s cosmic significance.

---

# Core Myths

### The Light That Turns  
The Everlight is said to echo the first cosmic breath.  
Its rotation is a reminder that harmony is motion.

### The Three Voices  
- Z: the harmonic  
- Pillars: the tectonic  
- Tinies: the chaotic  

Together, they form the universe’s chord.

### The Hidden World  
The Tinies believe they were chosen.  
The narrator believes they were an accident.  
Z believes both can be true.

### The Eternal Bakery  
A symbol of creation, destruction, and snacks.  
Its fires are considered sacred by some.  
Mostly they are just fires.

---

# Summary

The mythology binds the cosmic and the comedic — the heart of the Everlight universe.
