# Scene 03 — Z Intervenes  
_Version 1.0_

## Purpose

This scene introduces Z as a calming cosmic presence who tries to mediate between the narrator and the Tinies.  
It also establishes the comedic dynamic: Z reveres the Tinies, the Tinies sass Z, and the narrator is caught in the middle.

---

# Script (Draft)

*(Tinies are still shouting through the stolen device.)*

**Tiny #1:**  
“WE DEMAND SNACKS!”

**Tiny #2:**  
“AND A MEETING WITH MANAGEMENT!”

*(A harmonic, resonant tone fills the soundscape.)*

**Z (calm, cosmic):**  
“Little Ones…  
You are heard.”

*(Tinies gasp.)*

**Tiny #3:**  
“IT’S THE BIG GLOWY ONE!”

**Z:**  
“You shine with purpose.  
But perhaps…  
Return the device to the narrator.”

**Tiny #1:**  
“NO.  
WE LIKE THIS ONE.”

**Narrator:**  
“Z, thank you.  
Please explain to them—”

**Z:**  
“They are sacred custodians, Narrator.  
Their choices must be honoured.”

**Narrator:**  
“They have set fire to the bakery.”

**Z:**  
“Sacred flames.”

*(Tinies cheer.)*

---

# Notes

- Z’s tone is serene and poetic.  
- The Tinies adore Z but do not obey.  
- The narrator is losing control.  
- This scene deepens the mythic‑comedic contrast.
