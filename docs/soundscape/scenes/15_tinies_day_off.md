# Scene 15 — The Tinies’ Day Off  
_Version 1.0_

## Purpose

This scene explores what happens when the Tinies attempt to take a collective day off.  
Predictably, it becomes more stressful than a normal workday.  
It’s sweet, chaotic, and reveals their inability to relax.

---

# Script (Draft)

*(Soft morning ambience. Tiny birds chirp. A gentle hum from the Everlight.)*

**Tiny #1 (yawning):**  
“GOOD MORNING, EVERYONE!  
IT’S OUR DAY OFF!”

*(Tinies cheer weakly.)*

**Tiny #2:**  
“WHAT DO WE DO ON A DAY OFF?”

**Tiny #3:**  
“WE…  
RELAX?”

*(Tinies gasp as if hearing a forbidden word.)*

---

### ☀️ **Attempt #1: Relaxation**

*(Tinies lie down on tiny towels. Peaceful music plays.)*

**Tiny #4:**  
“I DON’T LIKE THIS.  
IT’S TOO QUIET.”

**Tiny #2:**  
“WHAT IF SOMETHING IS ON FIRE?”

**Tiny #1:**  
“NOTHING IS ON FIRE.”

*(Beat. A tiny alarm goes off.)*

**Tiny #3:**  
“THE BAKERY IS ON FIRE!”

**Narrator:**  
“Of course it is.”

---

### ☀️ **Attempt #2: A Picnic**

*(Tinies gather with snacks. Birds chirp. It’s almost peaceful.)*

**Tiny #5:**  
“THIS IS NICE.”

**Tiny #6:**  
“YES.  
VERY CALM.”

*(A train horn blares. A tiny train zooms past.)*

**Pip:**  
“DON’T MIND US!  
JUST TESTING THE EMERGENCY BRAKES!”

*(A crash. Tinies scream.)*

**Narrator:**  
“Why are you testing them today?”

**Pip:**  
“BECAUSE IT’S OUR DAY OFF!”

---

### ☀️ **Attempt #3: Doing Absolutely Nothing**

*(Tinies sit in silence. A full five seconds pass.)*

**Tiny #2:**  
“I CAN’T DO THIS.  
I NEED TO FILE SOMETHING.”

**Tiny #1:**  
“NO!  
NO PAPERWORK TODAY!”

**Tiny #2:**  
“JUST ONE FORM!”

**Tiny #1:**  
“NO FORMS!”

*(Tinies begin shaking from withdrawal.)*

---

### ☀️ **Z Intervenes**

*(A warm harmonic tone fills the space.)*

**Z:**  
“Little Ones…  
Rest is sacred.  
Stillness is a gift.”

**Tiny #3:**  
“CAN WE RETURN IT?”

**Z:**  
“No.”

---

### ☀️ **The Pillars Attempt to Help**

*(A deep tectonic rumble.)*

**Pillar:**  
“WE…  
SUGGEST…  
MEDITATION.”

**Tiny #4:**  
“WE TRIED THAT.  
WE SET THE BAKERY ON FIRE.”

**Narrator:**  
“How?”

**Tiny #4:**  
“WE DON’T KNOW!”

---

### ☀️ **Final Attempt: Group Nap**

*(Tinies pile into a cosy heap. Soft snoring. Peace at last.)*

**Narrator (softly):**  
“Oh.  
They’re actually resting.”

*(A tiny explosion in the distance.)*

**Tiny #1 (half‑asleep):**  
“IT’S FINE…  
WE’RE OFF DUTY…”

*(They all fall back asleep.)*

**Z (warmly):**  
“Even chaos must rest.”

---

# Notes

- This scene is gentle, funny, and endearing.  
- Tinies are incapable of relaxing without causing chaos.  
- Z is proud of them.  
- The narrator is bewildered.  
- The Pillars take meditation far too seriously.
