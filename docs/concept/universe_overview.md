# THE SOURCE — Universe Overview  
_Version 1.0_

## Introduction

**THE SOURCE** is a multi‑layered conceptual universe built around **The Everlight**, a luminous kinetic sculpture that houses an entire hidden civilisation and a mythic cosmology. This document provides a high‑level overview of the universe, its entities, its narrative logic, and the relationships between its layers.

The universe operates on three intertwined planes:

1. **The Physical Plane** — the sculpture itself  
2. **The Subterranean Plane** — the Tinies’ civilisation  
3. **The Mythic Plane** — Z, the Pillars, and the cosmic framework  

These planes overlap through the soundscape, creating a hybrid experience of art, comedy, myth, and bureaucracy.

---

## The Three Planes of the Universe

### 1. The Physical Plane  
This is what visitors see:

- The Everlight sculpture  
- Rotating rods  
- Internal illumination  
- Architectural elegance  
- A serene, meditative presence  

This plane is intentionally calm and mythic.  
It is the “face” of the universe.

### 2. The Subterranean Plane  
This is what visitors hear:

- The Tinies’ civilisation  
- Their homes, trains, tools, gossip, arguments  
- Their bureaucracy and endless insurance claim  
- Their comedic chaos  
- Their interactions with visitors and staff  

This plane is hidden physically but fully alive sonically.

### 3. The Mythic Plane  
This is the cosmic layer:

- **Z**, the Everlight’s central consciousness  
- **The Pillars**, ancient structural spirits  
- Their reverence for the Tinies  
- Their slow, resonant, ceremonial speech  
- Their mythic interpretation of the Everlight’s purpose  

This plane gives the universe its spiritual and symbolic weight.

---

## Core Entities

### The Everlight  
The central kinetic sculpture.  
A cosmic engine.  
A serene visual anchor.

### The Tinies  
A tiny, squeaky, bureaucratic civilisation trapped beneath the Everlight due to an unresolved insurance claim.  
They are the comedic heart of the universe.

### Z  
The Everlight’s internal consciousness.  
Calm, wise, reverent toward the Tinies.

### The Pillars  
Ancient structural spirits.  
Deep, slow, ceremonial.  
They honour the Tinies as sacred custodians.

### The Narrator  
A dignified voice attempting to explain the exhibit.  
Constantly interrupted by the Tinies.

---

## The Central Myth: The Dropped Amazeball

The universe begins with a single event:

**The Amazeball was dropped during installation.**

This triggered:

- a catastrophic insurance claim  
- blame passed between all parties  
- bureaucratic limbo  
- the accidental creation of the Tinies  
- their eternal entrapment beneath the Everlight  

This myth fuels:

- the Tinies’ frustration  
- their bureaucracy  
- their comedy  
- their interactions with staff  
- their reverence (and annoyance) from Z and the Pillars  

It is the universe’s comedic and narrative engine.

---

## Soundscape as the Bridge Between Planes

The soundscape is the connective tissue of the universe.

Through headphones, visitors hear:

- the Tinies’ ultrasonic squeals  
- their modulated voices (after stealing the narrator’s device)  
- Z’s cosmic resonance  
- the Pillars’ tectonic speech  
- the subterranean village ambience  
- the narrator’s attempts at order  

The soundscape reveals the hidden layers of the universe.

## Interactions and Dynamics

### Tinies ↔ Visitors  
The Tinies:

- heckle  
- warn  
- gossip  
- complain  
- flirt  
- panic  
- perform  

They treat visitors as giants wandering above their village.

### Tinies ↔ Staff  
The Tinies:

- demand meetings  
- unionise staff  
- accuse them of sabotage  
- beg for help with the claim  

### Tinies ↔ Z  
Z reveres them.  
They sass Z.

### Tinies ↔ Pillars  
The Pillars honour them.  
They mock the Pillars.

### Narrator ↔ Tinies  
The narrator tries to maintain dignity.  
The Tinies destroy it.

## Universe Summary

**THE SOURCE** is a layered conceptual universe where a serene kinetic sculpture contains a chaotic, bureaucratic micro‑civilisation revered by cosmic entities. The soundscape reveals the hidden world beneath the sculpture, blending myth, comedy, bureaucracy, and immersive storytelling into a single experience.

This document provides the high‑level map of the universe.
