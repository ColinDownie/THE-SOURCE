# Material Specifications  
_Version 1.0_

## Purpose

This document defines the materials used in the Everlight sculpture to ensure durability, safety, and aesthetic consistency.

---

# Structural Materials

### Rods
- anodised aluminium  
- lightweight  
- corrosion‑resistant  
- satin finish  

### Bearings
- sealed steel bearings  
- low‑friction  
- long‑life  

### Plinth
- powder‑coated steel frame  
- hardwood or composite exterior  
- anti‑tip design  

---

# Lighting Materials

### LEDs
- warm white  
- high CRI  
- low heat output  

### Diffusion Layer
- frosted acrylic  
- even light distribution  

---

# Acoustic Materials

### Internal Dampening
- vibration‑absorbing foam  
- acoustic isolation pads  

---

# Summary

Materials are chosen for longevity, safety, and the Everlight’s signature serene aesthetic.
