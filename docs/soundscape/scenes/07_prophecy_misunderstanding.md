# Scene 07 — The Prophecy Misunderstanding  
_Version 1.0_

## Purpose

This scene escalates the Pillars’ obsession with omens and prophecies, while the Tinies and narrator desperately try to explain that nothing mystical is happening.

---

# Script (Draft)

*(A deep rumble — the Pillars awaken.)*

**Pillar:**  
“WE… HAVE… SEEN… A SIGN…”

**Narrator:**  
“Please, not again.”

**Pillar:**  
“A TINY…  
HAS MISPLACED…  
A TOOL.”

**Tiny #1:**  
“IT WAS ONE TIME!”

**Pillar:**  
“THE PROPHECY…  
BEGINS.”

**Narrator:**  
“There is no prophecy.”

**Tiny #2:**  
“WHAT PROPHECY?  
WHY WEREN’T WE TOLD?”

**Pillar:**  
“THE PROPHECY…  
THAT WAS FORETOLD…  
WHEN THE AMAZEBALL…  
WAS DROPPED.”

*(Tinies gasp.)*

**Tiny #3:**  
“THAT WAS AN ACCIDENT!”

**Z (calm, harmonic):**  
“Pillars…  
Perhaps we need not interpret every mishap as destiny.”

**Pillar:**  
“WE… RESPECTFULLY…  
DISAGREE.”

---

# Notes

- The Pillars are dramatic and slow.  
- The Tinies panic at the idea of a prophecy.  
- The narrator tries to keep things grounded.  
- Z mediates with cosmic patience.
