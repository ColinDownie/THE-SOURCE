# Scene 08 — Rail Network Meltdown  
_Version 1.0_

## Purpose

This scene reveals the Tinies’ subterranean rail system in full catastrophic collapse.  
It’s loud, chaotic, and full of blame.

---

# Script (Draft)

*(Tiny train whistles. Screaming. Metal clanks.)*

**Pip (rail operator):**  
“THE 4:15 TO PLATFORM B IS OFF THE RAILS!”

**Pop (other operator):**  
“IT WASN’T MY FAULT!”

**Pip:**  
“YOU WERE DRIVING!”

**Pop:**  
“YOU SAID TURN LEFT!”

**Pip:**  
“I SAID *DON’T* TURN LEFT!”

*(A tiny crash. More squeaks.)*

**Narrator:**  
“Is everyone alright down there?”

**Tiny #3:**  
“NO!  
THE TRAIN IS IN THE BAKERY!”

**Narrator:**  
“Why is everything always in the bakery?”

*(Z enters, calm.)*

**Z:**  
“Little Ones…  
Your journeys take unexpected paths.”

**Pip:**  
“THE PATH IS THROUGH A WALL!”
