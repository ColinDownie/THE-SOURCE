# Scene 20 — The Bakery Reopens  
_Version 1.0_

## Purpose

A comedic post‑credits scene.  
The bakery, after countless fires, finally reopens…  
for about five seconds.

---

# Script (Draft)

*(A triumphant tiny trumpet fanfare.)*

**Tiny Baker:**  
“WELCOME, EVERYONE!  
THE BAKERY…  
IS OFFICIALLY REOPENED!”

*(Tinies cheer wildly.)*

**Narrator:**  
“Oh!  
That’s wonderful news.”

*(A tiny spark. A tiny whoosh. A tiny explosion.)*

**Tiny Baker:**  
“THE BAKERY…  
IS OFFICIALLY CLOSED!”

*(Tinies scream. Narrator sighs.)*

**Z (warmly):**  
“Some cycles are eternal.”
