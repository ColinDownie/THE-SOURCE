# Scene 04 — The Pillars Speak  
_Version 1.0_

## Purpose

This scene introduces the Pillars — the ancient, tectonic spirits who support the Everlight.  
Their slow, booming voices contrast with the Tinies’ chaos and the narrator’s exhaustion.

---

# Script (Draft)

*(A deep, resonant hum fills the soundscape.)*

**Pillar (slow, seismic):**  
“WE… HAVE… OBSERVED… A SIGN…”

**Narrator:**  
“Oh no.  
Please don’t start with omens.”

**Pillar:**  
“THE LITTLE KEEPERS…  
HAVE DROPPED… A SPOON.”

**Narrator:**  
“It was an accident.”

*(Tinies squeak indignantly.)*

**Tiny #1:**  
“IT WAS A VERY IMPORTANT SPOON!”

**Pillar:**  
“THE PROPHECY…  
UNFOLDS.”

**Narrator:**  
“There is no prophecy.”

**Pillar:**  
“THERE IS ALWAYS… A PROPHECY.”

*(Z enters with a harmonic tone.)*

**Z:**  
“Pillars… perhaps we need not declare omens today.”

**Pillar:**  
“WE… DISAGREE.”

---

# Notes

- The Pillars speak extremely slowly.  
- The narrator tries to keep things grounded.  
- The Tinies escalate the drama.  
- Z attempts to mediate.
